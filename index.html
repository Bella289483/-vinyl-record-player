Deca Joins

Deca Joins (stylized as deca joins) is a Taiwanese indie rock band that formed in 2013 in Taipei.

Click to continue

The band currently consists of four members:

  • Zheng Jingru - vocals, guitar
  • Yang Shanghua - guitar
  • Xie Junyan - bass
  • Chen Huanggu - drums

Their music has been described by critics as "indie," "grunge," and "lo-fi."
